About Us

Coastal Critter Craft Co is a small local business creating handcrafted painted bird houses for Aussie wildlife across the Eyre Peninsula. Every house is made using natural timber, simple designs, and bush‑to‑coast inspiration that fits perfectly into local yards.

We focus on honest, handmade builds — nothing extreme, just quality bird houses made with care.
